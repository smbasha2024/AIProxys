General Questions

What exactly are Agentic AI Bots?

Agentic AI Bots are advanced artificial intelligence systems that can perceive, plan, and execute multi-step processes to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional chatbots that follow scripted interactions, our Agentic AI Bots can reason, make decisions, and adapt to new situations using large language models and specialized tools.

How are your solutions different from standard automation tools?

Traditional automation tools follow predefined rules and workflows. Our Agentic AI solutions incorporate reasoning, adaptability, and decision-making capabilities. They can handle unstructured tasks, make judgment calls, and learn from interactions, making them suitable for complex business processes that require cognitive abilities.

What industries do you primarily serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including healthcare, education, e-commerce, finance, hospitality, and enterprise services. Our solutions are particularly effective in sectors that involve customer interaction, process coordination, knowledge management, and repetitive cognitive tasks.

Do you provide pre-built bots or customized solutions?

Both! We offer ready-made bots for quick deployment and fully personalized AI agents with custom workflows, UI, languages, and integrations.

Services & Solutions

What does your business operations study involve?

Our business operations study is a comprehensive analysis of your current workflows, pain points, and opportunities. It includes process mapping, efficiency metrics evaluation, stakeholder interviews, and technology stack assessment. We deliver a detailed report with actionable insights and specific recommendations for where Agentic AI solutions can deliver the most value.

Do you offer industry-specific solutions?

Yes, we offer both industry-specific solutions and cross-industry applications. Our pre-built agents are tailored for specific sectors like healthcare (virtual receptionists, appointment scheduling), education (CBSE tutors), and customer service (support agents). We also develop custom solutions for unique business needs across industries.

Can we customize your pre-built agents?

Absolutely. All our pre-built agents are designed with customization in mind. You can tailor the knowledge base, conversation flows, user interface, integration points, and even the personality of the agent to match your specific requirements, branding, and operational processes.

What makes your pre-built agents unique?

Our agents deliver immediate business value with features like virtual reception, appointment scheduling, lead management, and inventory automation—while allowing full customization.

Implementation

How long does it typically take to implement an Agentic AI solution?

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity and customization. For pre-built solutions with moderate customization, implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks. For fully custom solutions involving complex integrations, the process can take 8-12 weeks. Our phased approach ensures you start seeing value quickly while we continue to refine and expand capabilities.

What integrations do your solutions support?

Our solutions support integrations with a wide range of systems including CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), ERPs (SAP, Oracle), communication platforms (Slack, Microsoft Teams), database systems, and custom APIs. We also offer pre-built connectors for common healthcare, education, and business applications.

How do you ensure data security and privacy?

Data security is paramount in our solutions. We implement encryption both in transit and at rest, adhere to strict access controls, and comply with relevant regulations (GDPR, HIPAA where applicable). For highly sensitive data, we offer on-premise deployment options. All our solutions undergo rigorous security testing before deployment.
