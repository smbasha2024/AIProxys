Our Policies & Guidelines

Transparent guidelines for the ethical use of our Agentic AI solutions

AI Ethics Policy

At IntelliAgent, we are committed to developing and deploying AI solutions that are ethical, transparent, and beneficial to society. Our ethics policy includes:

  • Transparency: We clearly communicate the capabilities and limitations of our AI agents
  • Fairness: Our algorithms are designed to avoid bias and ensure equitable treatment
  • Privacy: We implement robust data protection measures to safeguard user information
  • Accountability: We maintain clear lines of responsibility for AI system behavior
  • Human oversight: Our systems are designed to work alongside human operators

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

We take the confidentiality of your business information seriously. Our standard NDA includes:

  • Protection of proprietary business information shared during consultations
  • Confidentiality of custom AI solutions developed for your organization
  • Data processing and security protocols for sensitive information
  • Clear terms regarding intellectual property rights for developed solutions
  • Duration of confidentiality obligations beyond the project timeline

We are happy to review and sign your organization's NDA if you have specific requirements.

Data Handling Policy

Our approach to data management ensures security and compliance:

  • Data minimization: We only collect data essential for project requirements
  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access controls: Strict role-based access to client data within our organization
  • Retention policy: Clear guidelines on data retention and secure deletion procedures
  • Compliance: Adherence to relevant data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Responsible Use Policy

To ensure our AI solutions are used appropriately, we require clients to agree to:

  • Not use our AI agents for illegal activities or content generation
  • Implement appropriate human oversight for critical decisions
  • Regularly review and audit AI system outputs for accuracy and appropriateness
  • Not attempt to reverse engineer or modify our AI systems without authorization
  • Provide transparency to end-users when they are interacting with an AI system

Support & Maintenance Policy

Our commitment to ongoing client success includes:

  • Service Level Agreement (SLA): Guaranteed response times for critical issues
  • Regular updates: Security patches and feature improvements
  • Training: Comprehensive training for your team on using and maintaining AI solutions
  • Documentation: Detailed technical and user documentation
  • Exit assistance: Support during transition if services are discontinued
