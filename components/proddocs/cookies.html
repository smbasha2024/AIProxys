Understanding how we use cookies to enhance your experience

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users, which helps us provide you with a better experience and allows us to improve our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for the operation of our website, including logging in and processing payments Session
Analytical/Performance Cookies Allow us to recognize and count visitors and see how they move around our site 6-12 months
Functionality Cookies Remember choices you make to provide enhanced, more personal features 6-12 months
Targeting Cookies Record your visit, pages visited, and links followed to make advertising more relevant 3-6 months

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. The Help feature on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether.

Please note that if you disable cookies, you may not be able to access all features of our website and some parts of the site may not function properly.

These third-party cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these external services.

Cookie Consent

Your continued use of our website without changing your settings will be interpreted as consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
