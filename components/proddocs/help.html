Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get support for our Agentic AI solutions

General

  • What are Agentic AI Bots?

    Agentic AI Bots are advanced artificial intelligence systems that can perceive, reason, and take action to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional chatbots that simply respond to queries, our Agentic AI Bots can initiate actions, make decisions, and work autonomously within defined parameters.

  • How are your AI solutions different from standard automation?

    Our Agentic AI solutions go beyond simple rule-based automation by incorporating reasoning capabilities, adaptability to new situations, and the ability to make context-aware decisions. They can handle complex workflows that would typically require human intervention.

  • What industries do you serve?

    We serve a wide range of industries including healthcare, education, hospitality, retail, finance, and manufacturing. Our solutions are adaptable to virtually any sector that can benefit from intelligent automation and AI assistance.

Implementation

  • How long does it take to implement an Agentic AI solution?

    Implementation time varies based on the complexity of the solution and the level of customization required. Pre-built solutions can often be deployed in 2-4 weeks, while fully custom solutions may take 2-6 months from discovery to deployment.

  • What integration capabilities do your solutions offer?

    Our solutions can integrate with a wide variety of systems including CRMs, ERPs, databases, APIs, and legacy systems. We use standard protocols and can develop custom connectors when needed to ensure seamless integration with your existing technology stack.

  • Do you provide training for our team?

    Yes, we provide comprehensive training for both technical and non-technical staff. This includes administrator training for managing the AI systems, and end-user training for those who will interact with the AI agents regularly.

Security & Compliance

  • How do you ensure data security?

    We implement multiple layers of security including encryption of data in transit and at rest, strict access controls, regular security audits, and compliance with industry-standard security protocols. We can also deploy solutions on-premises or in private clouds for additional security if required.

  • Are your solutions compliant with regulations like GDPR?

    Yes, our solutions are designed with compliance in mind. We adhere to GDPR, HIPAA (for healthcare applications), and other relevant regulations based on your industry and location. We can also customize solutions to meet specific compliance requirements.

Support & Maintenance

  • What support options do you offer?

    We offer multiple support tiers including standard business hours support, 24/7 critical issue support, and dedicated account management. Our support includes troubleshooting, performance monitoring, and regular system health checks.

  • How do you handle updates and improvements?

    We regularly release updates that include security patches, performance improvements, and new features. These updates are tested in staging environments before being deployed to production systems. For custom solutions, we coordinate updates with clients to minimize disruption.
